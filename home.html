Transforming the Instructional Landscape (TIL) is a hub of collaboration, community and learning space design that sits at the crossroads of multiple departments at UofT.
Stakeholder engagement is central to TIL, and we are always looking for instructors and staff at U of T to become involved in our work. Those involved with TIL can receive support in exploring technology, learning methods and learning space innovation.
TIL not only focuses on classroom renovations and technology enhancement, but it takes a more holistic approach to exploring best practices for learning space design, whether they be online or in-person.
